Grab a coffee, enjoy great music, and hit the rocks in Utrecht.

How to have a great day in Utrecht

Enjoy a good cup of coffee and relax

Het Muzieklokaal serves great coffee accompanied by calming classical music.

Attend a concert of your favorite artist

TivoliVredenburg offers many concerts by both well-known and obscure artists in many genres.

Do some rock climbing!

Boulderhal Energiehaven takes care of all your bouldering needs, whether you're a beginner or a well-seasoned climber.
